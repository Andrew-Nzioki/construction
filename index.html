About us

About Us Company Overview Jicago Contractors Limited is among the resourceful and innovative building and construction company within the country. It is registered with the National Construction Authority, therefore qualifying it to conduct construction activities and change the face of building industry across the country. The firm has been in existence for a period of 10 years under the management of Collins Jirenge who is the manager though its formalization came about in the year 2023.

Jicago Contractors Limited headquarters are located in Lafforet House, along Mayor Road, off Magadi Road in Ongata Rongai Town. Servies Offered Jicago has executed to completion several projects Tuala, Matasia, Rankau Kisii, South B, South C. Ngong, Karen, Kisumu, Mombasa, Upper Hill in Nairobi and its environs.
